Winch Pre-Operation Checklist Reference — Drum Winch OM p.25

The following checks are mandated by the PistenBully 400 W Drum Winch Operating Manual before each winch operation session. These are for reference and are included in the Mechanical Check form above.

Step 1
Remove snow and ice from winch
Step 2
Check winch fasteners (1) — Tightening torque MA = 500 Nm
Step 3
Check operation of slewing-gear holding brake
Step 4
Check hydraulic lines & connectors — free of leaks and chafing
Step 5
Check cable guide arm moves freely on windlass
Step 6
Clean viewing screen for winch cable winding
Step 7
Check operation of safety lock (3)
Step 8
Check ease of movement — cable relay rollers in winch boom and cable guide arm
Step 9
Grease guide roll — nipple (9) and shaft (8) with Aviacal 2 LD (KP2K-30 DIN 51502)
